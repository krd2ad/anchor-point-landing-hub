# Anchor Point Lending — Single-Page Website

## Overview

A clean, high-trust single-page website for a private real estate lending company. Navy/blue/white color palette with premium financial-services aesthetic.

## Structure

### 1. Sticky Header

- Logo (uploaded anchor icon) + "Anchor Point Lending" on the left
- Smooth-scroll nav links: Home, Solutions, Team, About, Contact
- "Get Started" CTA button on the right
- Transparent/white background with subtle shadow on scroll

### 2. Hero Section

- Full-width dark navy background with subtle gradient overlay
- Bold headline: "Capital Solutions to Move Your Projects Forward"
- Supporting subtext about flexible lending, speed, and partnership
- Two CTA buttons: "Explore Solutions" and "Contact Us"
- Clean, confident typography with strong visual weight

### 3. Solutions Section

- "Our Solutions" heading
- 4-card grid (2×2 on desktop, stacked on mobile)
- Cards: Bridge Loans, Commercial, Fix and Flip, Ground-Up Construction
- Each with icon, title, short description, and subtle "Learn More" text
- Light background, polished card styling with shadows

### 4. About Section

- "About Anchor Point Lending" heading
- Split layout: text block on left, visual panel on right
- Placeholder company story, mission, and lending philosophy
- Professional, established tone

### 5. Team Section

- "Our Team" heading
- Two elegant profile cards side by side
- Sam and Rivers with placeholder avatars
- Each card: name, title, phone, email, LinkedIn icons
- Modern card design with clear visual hierarchy

### 6. Footer

- Minimal dark bar
- "© 2026 Anchor Point Lending. All rights reserved."

## Design System

- **Colors**: Navy (#1a2332), Blue (#2563eb), White, Light gray backgrounds
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif, strong heading hierarchy
- **Cards**: Subtle shadows, rounded corners, hover states
- **Spacing**: Generous padding between sections
- **Responsive**: Desktop-first, fully mobile-friendly
- Logo image copied into project assets
